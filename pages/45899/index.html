# Duration of matrix matrix calculation in prefilling stage

  1. matrix projection for Q (~90us) 2,3) matrix projection for K and V (~34us and ~30us) Since it is mult head, the compute MAC for Q is 4 times higher than K and V
  2. Attention & Projection to value with attention (~90us)
  3. FFN gate (~286us)
  4. FFN up (~286us)
  5. FFN down (~250us)
