# why it is 6 fold?

reference (opens new window)

In short, for backward pass, the back propagation loss needs to be multiplied by weight to pass get the input-loss for privous layer. It also needs to multiplied by the input of this layer to get the derivative of the weights for updating the weight.

Those two above is multiplication operation, it also needs another two add operation to sum up input-loss for privous layer and update the weigth, one for each.
