## prefilling stage

### Duration of matrix matrix calculation in prefilling stage

1) matrix projection for Q   (~90us)
2,3) matrix projection for K and V  (~34us and ~30us)
   Since it is mult head, the compute MAC for Q is 4 times higher than K and V
4) Attention & Projection to value with attention (~90us)
5) FFN gate   (~286us)
6) FFN up     (~286us)
7) FFN down   (~250us)
